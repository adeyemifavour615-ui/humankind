Building a positive platform

We’re championing change to make the internet a safer, more positive and more inspirational place.

Prioritising people

Through resources and initiatives that promote well-being, belonging and opportunity, we work to create pathways to success for our employees and the broader communities that we serve.

Protecting our planet

By measuring and reducing our greenhouse gas emissions, investing in renewable electricity and partnering with organisations that share our goals, we’re working to protect our planet through meaningful climate action.

Progress with integrity

As we advance our commitment to fostering positive outcomes for our people, platform and planet, our governance framework sets the foundation for a culture of accountability and ethical conduct.
